Deposit & Withdrawal
YLG Bullion accepts the following payment methods:
  • KPay
  • WavePay
  • KBZ Bank
  • AYA Bank
  • CB Bank
  • USDT (TRC20)
  • USDT (ERC20)
Please contact Live Support or check the Deposit page for the current minimum deposit amount. Minimum amounts may vary by payment method.
Withdrawal requests are reviewed and processed by our admin team within 24 hours. Processing time may vary depending on your payment method and bank processing times.
Minimum withdrawal amount is $10.
Withdrawals may be rejected for the following reasons:
  • Incomplete or incorrect bank account details
  • KYC verification not completed
  • Account is frozen or under review
  • Withdrawal amount exceeds available balance
If your withdrawal is rejected, your balance will be refunded automatically. Contact Live Support for more details.
The Withdrawal PIN is a 4-digit security PIN required every time you make a withdrawal. To set it:
  • Go to Portfolio
  • Scroll to the Security section
  • Click "Set Withdrawal PIN"
  • Enter and confirm your 4-digit PIN
Keep your PIN safe. Do not share it with anyone.
Trading
YLG Bullion uses a BUY / SELL prediction system based on real-time gold (XAUUSD) prices:
  • Select your trade amount and duration
  • Choose BUY if you think gold will go up, or SELL if you think it will go down
  • Wait for the countdown to finish
  • If your prediction is correct, you win the payout. If not, you lose your trade amount
Trading involves risk. Only trade what you can afford to lose.
The minimum trade amount varies by tier level. You can view available trade tiers directly on the Trading page before placing a trade.
Your trade result depends on the gold price movement during your selected trade duration, not just whether gold went up or down overall. If you chose BUY but gold price dropped during your trade window, the trade is a loss — even if gold recovered after the trade ended.
No. Once a trade is confirmed, it cannot be cancelled or reversed. All trades are final. Please double-check your trade amount and direction before confirming.
KYC Verification
KYC (Know Your Customer) is required to:
  • Verify your identity and protect your account
  • Comply with anti-money laundering (AML) regulations
  • Enable withdrawal of funds from your account
KYC is a one-time process and your documents are stored securely.
You will need to submit a valid government-issued identity document such as:
  • National Identity Card (NIC)
  • Passport
  • Driving License
Please ensure the document is clear, valid, and not expired.
KYC verification is typically reviewed within 24–48 hours by our admin team. You will be notified once your verification is approved or if additional information is required.
